About Stem Academy

Our team participated in a UX redesign for the website recently known as "Girls4Sciene". The first order of business we tended to was running user tests on the original website so we could determine user pain points. From our resuslts we created a user persona named Natasha. You can learn more about her in the "User Research" section below. After our persona was complete we ran a competitive Analysis and created a mood board to get ideas for our rebranding of "Girls4Science (shown in "User Research" section below). After rebranding we were each assigned a section of the website to wireframe using Invision. You can see our sketches and iterations in the "Wireframe" section below. Once we were in agreeance with the wireframes we can designing and protoyping in Adobe XD (shown below).

Final Thoughts

My teammates and I really wanted to help to this organization reach as many girls as possible. The statistics of women and more specifically Black and Latina women employed in STEM fields are embarrassingly low. For a few short weeks I felt as if this class project could really make a difference. Our next steps would be to get our redesign in the hands of the donators. I would love for the program to be able to hold more than 25 girls at a time. Hence the reason there are large donation footers on several pages and many reasons to give back listed on the donation page.
